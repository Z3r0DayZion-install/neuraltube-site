Product summary

Built for people who need signal, context, and control.

What NeuralTube is

A desktop video intelligence terminal with intentionally separate Watcher and Sovereign modes for comparison, review, contradiction tracking, and focused analysis.

Who it is for

Researchers, investigators, technical operators, and disciplined users who care more about coherent review than passive scrolling.

What problem it solves

It replaces opaque engagement-driven browsing with a local-first environment designed for deliberate inspection, narrative cross-checking, and release-grade accountability.

Why it is different

A different operating model than normal video platforms.

Intentional watching

Review on purpose instead of getting dragged by a feed.

NeuralTube is designed around operator control, not recommendation-loop addiction. The product direction stays aligned with analysis and clarity.

Analysis over noise

Cross-check narratives instead of skimming fragments.

The product surface is built around comparison, overlays, and inspection rather than maximizing passive time-on-device.
